Good Eats for All

From Breweries to Fine Dining, Cleveland has a great food scene.

Breakfast Spots

Luna Bakery and Cafe

Located in one of the best spots in Cleveland Heights, Luna is the perfect spot to stop for coffee a pastry. From crepes to breakfast burritos to all the coffee you can think of, Luna is easily one of the best places to spend your morning. You're never short of options at Luna, check out their menu here.

Heck's Cafe

If you find yourself in the heart of Ohio City one morning, be sure to dine at the classic Heck's Cafe. It's staple of the city that offers one of the most delicious brunch menus that will leave you dreaming of their french toast. Check out their menu here.

Lunch Spots

Ohio City Burrito

Easy, cheap, and always delicious, OCB never fails to deliver the best burrito. If you're tired of that sad burrito you are getting at Chipotle, you must stop here and your life will be changed forever. Visit one of their many locations within the city today, and head on over to their menu here .

Tommy's Restaurant

For all the vegans, vegetarians, and everyone in between, Tommy's is the perfect spot to eat. In Coventry Village close to the city's Little Italy and Lakeview Cemetery not only is it a great location, but their food never fails. Try their falafel, turkey burger, or one of their great milkshakes. You have a hard time choosing after reading their menu.

Dinner Spots

Edwin's Restaurant

For a classic French meal in Shaker Heights, visit Edwin's. Not only do they offer an incredible dining experience, but the restaurant and its owner are committed to employing formerly incarcerated individuals and entering them in a leadership institute. Feel good about where you're spending your money and treat yourself to an amazing meal at Edwin's. For their menu click here.

Lindy's Lakehouse

If you are looking for a great atmosphere with reliable eats, head over to Lindy's Lakehouse located in the always fun Cleveland Flats along the Cuyahoga River. Perfect for those who enjoy seafood and even for those who don't, you don't want to pass up a trip to Lindy's. Access their menu here.
