Cleveland, Ohio

Though many may disagree, Cleveland, Ohio is one of the greatest places on Earth.

The city was founded in 1796 at the mouth of the Cuyahoga River (yes, the one the caught on fire –– multiple times) by Moses Cleaveland. Now, it's one of the cities on the rise you will sure want to check out. Continue on through the site for sights to see and places to eat in the wonderful city of Cleveland.
